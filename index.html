Our Roots

Straight from Panabo City

It all comes down to the banana. We are proud to source our main ingredient from **Panabo City**, known as the "Banana Capital" of the Philippines. The unique soil and climate there produce bananas that are naturally sweeter and perfect for frying.

What started with a simple family recipe and a frying pan has grown into a local favorite. We haven't changed our process: hand-selected bananas, fresh oil, and a lot of love. Now, you can get that authentic Panabo taste delivered right to your door.
